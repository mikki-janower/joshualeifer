XJoshua Leifer is currently based in Brooklyn, New York.
He's a spunky ex-vegan with a heart of gold and abs of steel. He can regenerate lost limbs, like a starfish. He is best known for writing the Communist Manifesto (1848), though he has also been credited for the discovery of Radium (1898).

Leifer has never staged a glamorous Casino heist, though he has alleged that plans are in the works for early 2021. For business inquiries, throw a pebble at the nearest window in hopes that it will find him resting his elbows on the sill, gazing out across the overcast city towards a brighter horizon.
