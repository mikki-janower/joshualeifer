XJoshua Leifer is an editor, reporter, translator, and reluctant art director.
Prior to Jewish Currents, Joshua was an associate editor at Dissent, where he is now an editor-at-large. He was also previously an editor at +972 Magazine and remains an occasional contributor. He has reported from Jerusalem, Tel Aviv, Ramallah, Budapest, Berlin, and New York. He is currently based in Brooklyn. Feel free to send tips, feedback, and inquiries, including translation requests (Hebrew to English).
